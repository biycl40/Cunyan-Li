## About

I am an interdisciplinary researcher building GeoAI methods for urban science, human-environment interaction, and 3D urban analytics. My work connects **street view imagery**, **remote sensing**, **3D city models**, and **multimodal large language models** to make urban environments more measurable, interpretable, and actionable.

Before joining Texas A&M, I earned my M.S. in Spatial Data Science at the **University of Southern California** and worked as a Research Assistant at **HKUST(GZ) AI4City Lab**, **Harvard Spatial Data Lab**, and **The Hong Kong Polytechnic University**.

I am open to collaboration on GeoAI, spatial analysis, urban perception, street-view intelligence, remote sensing, and 3D urban space.

## Research Interests

- Street view imagery and urban perception
